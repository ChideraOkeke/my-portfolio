Internship

I had an internship program in the year 2017. It lasted 6 weeks. I was an intern at Outreach Women and Children's Hospital located in FESTAC town Lagos State, Nigeria. I shadowed the nurses for three weeks and then the doctors for the remaining three weeks. On my first day, I was given a tour and shown how the hospital works. I worked in the pre-natal and natal unit for a while. I learnt how to use the blood pressure machine, the syringe pump, heart monitors and incubators. I learnt the term triage and how it is applied in their hospital procedure. Triage the assignment of degrees of urgency to wounds or illnesses to decide the order of treatment of a large number of patients or casualties. I also learnt how to check the basic vitals of a patient. I helped the nurses arrange the consultation rooms, carry out triage and check vitals of patients. I also helped by preparing drugs for injections. I made runs to the pharmacy and I also carried samples and specimen to the laboratory to be tested. I also worked in the pathology department of the hospital. Working in the hospital was very educative and fun too. I made me more happy about my career choice and made more driven too do more.
